Devil May Cry

Overview

Two millenniums ago, there was a war. Between the human world and the other... the Underworld.
But somebody from the Underworld woke up to justice, and stood up against this legion, alone. His name was Sparda.
Later, he quietly reigned the human world, and continued to preserve harmony, until his death. He became a legend.
The Legendary Dark Knight, Sparda.
